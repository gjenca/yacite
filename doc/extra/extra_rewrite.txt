**REWRITE FILE FORMAT (yaml):**

> a list of pairs of strings

    - [*pattern_string_1*,*replace_string_1*]
    - [*pattern_string_2*,*replace_string_2*]
    - ...
    - [*pattern_string_n*,*replace_string_n*]

**EXAMPLE REWRITE FILE**
    
    - ["Jenca","Jenča"]
    - ["Kuhr","Kühr"]
    - ["Kuehr","Kühr"]
